Course Code: EDLD681
Title: EDLD 681 Internship I
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/edld-education-leadership/600/edld-681
Credits: 2
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Internship I engages the candidate in mastering the entry-level knowledge, skills, dispositions, roles, and responsibilities of a superintendent. The internship engages the candidate in the foundations of school system administration: culture, teamwork, problem-solving, decision-making, and leadership. The candidate develops a certification portfolio using standards-based assessment data, evidence, and reflections on professional growth experiences.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Summarize knowledge, skills, strengths, and needs using performance indicators and rubrics.
2. Analyze the scope and depth of the problem-solving work of a school system CEO.
3. Demonstrate growth towards meeting the standards for superintendent certification.

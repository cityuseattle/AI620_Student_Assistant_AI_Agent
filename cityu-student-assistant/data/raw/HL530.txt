Course Code: HL530
Title: HL 530 Foundations of Healthcare Administration
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/hl-healthcare/500/hl-530
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
In this course, students will study key contextual and environmental factors that affect the practice of healthcare administration. Focus is made on the importance of culture, communication, and diversity. Students are introduced to the roles and responsibilities of the stakeholders in the field of healthcare. The significance of professional behaviors such as goal setting and time management are covered, as well as the attitudes and motivation required for success as a healthcare manager.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Analyze the importance of culture, communication, and diversity within healthcare administration.
Analyze the impact of attitude and motivation on the success of a healthcare manager.
Appraise the use of goal setting and time management for a healthcare leader.
Compare and contrast the roles of the stakeholders in the field of healthcare administration.

Course Code: MAL592
Title: MAL 592 Applied Leadership Analysis
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/mal-management-leadership/500/mal-592
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Incorporating leadership values, models, and theories, students will analyze leadership through personal-reflection, a cornerstone for successful leadership. Cementing their understanding of leadership, students will analyze their understanding of leadership, apply preferred leadership styles, and synthesize leadership values and concepts to employ a personalized approach to self-leadership.

Course Code: DBA610
Title: DBA 610 Financial Decision Making
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/dba-doctor-business-administration/600/dba-610
Credits: 6
Prerequisites: None

Description:
In this course, students examine macro-financial trends impacting today’s businesses and organizations. Emphasis is placed on financial concepts, models, and theories that can be applied to the identified challenges facing business leaders across industry sectors. Projects include presenting a comprehensive financial analysis, connecting emerging global challenges with internal decision-making, and making recommendations to fund a new strategic initiative or investment through capital acquisition, allocation, or structuring. By completing this course, students apply how to formulate business strategies through relevant course projects.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Analyze emerging global trends impacting business performance.
2. Analyze the capital and/or organizational structure of a variety of business organizations.
3. Evaluate the short- and long-term health of an organization.
4. Create strategic recommendations for improving organizational performance in a global context.

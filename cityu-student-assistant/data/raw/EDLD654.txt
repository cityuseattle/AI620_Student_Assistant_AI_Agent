Course Code: EDLD654
Title: EDLD 654 Organizational Leadership and Resource Management
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/edld-education-leadership/600/edld-654
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
In this course, candidates engage in learning to solve problems around complex organizational and resource management issues.  They investigate and debate soft and hard issues that when resolved are at the heart of every successful organization.  Candidates learn to focus on knowing one’s skills and styles and deploying them effectively in their work.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Use research-based strategies and tools to engage stakeholders in improving and managing a school system.
2. Demonstrate essential knowledge and skills for managing human and systems resources.
3. Develop the knowledge and skills to manage the instructional, physical, and organizational resources of a school system.

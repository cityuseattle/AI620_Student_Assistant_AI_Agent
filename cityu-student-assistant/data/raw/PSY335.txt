Course Code: PSY335
Title: PSY 335 Psychology of Personality *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/psy-psychology/300/psy-335
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: Only available in Canada

Description:
This course introduces the major theories of personality, which serve as a foundation for much research and practice in psychology. It explores key topics regarding the definition and development of personality, individual and cultural variations in personality, and the practical application of personality research findings.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Examine depth-oriented perspectives on personality (Depth Perspectives on Personality Paper).
2. Analyze personality development across the lifespan (The Evolution of Personality Paper).
3. Examine the evolutionary context of personality (The Evolving Self Presentation/Project).
4. Critique cultural differences in personality traits (The Evolving Self Presentation/Project).

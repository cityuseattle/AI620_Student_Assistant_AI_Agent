Course Code: COM315
Title: COM 315 Media and Society in the Digital Age (HU)
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/com-communications-revised/300/com-315
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: None

Description:
The digital landscape has transformed our social and work lives, community building, and identity management. Media is a source of information, entertainment, and persuasion in our everyday lives, and in the digital age the line between media producers and media consumers is increasingly blurred. This course is a comprehensive introduction to the effects of media communication processes and new media technologies on contemporary life. Students will explore the intertwined relationships between media and society.

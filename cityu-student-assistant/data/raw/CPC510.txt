Course Code: CPC510
Title: CPC 510 Professional Ethics and Law *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/cpc-counselling-psychology-canada/500/cpc-510
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course provides an introduction to ethical and legal codes and laws pertaining to therapeutic counselling practice. Areas of focus include practitioner responsibilities, liability, consent, confidentiality, dual relationships, and inter-professional communication. Students will explore the complex relationship between ethics, law, and the therapeutic alliance.

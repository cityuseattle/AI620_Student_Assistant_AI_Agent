Course Code: BAM495
Title: BAM 495 Strategic Business Management Degree Capstone *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/bam-bachelor-of-arts-management/400/bam-495
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: This course is the capstone for students in the Bachelor of Arts in Management-Organizational Excellence. This course is the last and final course block of a total four course block and should not be taken until the student has completed BAM 400, BAM 405, and BAM 410 or their equivalent.

Description:
In this course students will gain a broad foundational knowledge of the many facets of management by practicing effective communication skills, employing best practices in project management, and demonstrating their ability to lead and participate in diverse work teams. Students will apply research and critical thinking skills to develop creative, ethical solutions to a variety of business problems and to support professional and organizational development.

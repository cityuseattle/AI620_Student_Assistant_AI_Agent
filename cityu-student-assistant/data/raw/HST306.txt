Course Code: HST306
Title: HST 306 Modern World History (SS)
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/hst-history/300/hst-306
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course examines historical changes and processes in the 20th century and beyond. Providing a global perspective, the course will explore political, social, and economic changes that have impacted people and societies across the world

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Analyze historical ideas, issues, and events.
Evaluate the role of leaders in historical, political, and socioeconomic events.
Examine current events in historical context.

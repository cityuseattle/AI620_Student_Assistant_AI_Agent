Course Code: BSM415
Title: BSM 415 Strategic Logistics Management
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/bsm-business-major/400/bsm-415
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Superior logistics and supply chain management are critical to the success of many businesses. This course introduces students to the primary aspects of logistics and supply chain functions with an emphasis on the application of strategy to supply chain design and decision-making. Upon completion students will have a solid managerial understanding of logistics and its relationship to the organization.

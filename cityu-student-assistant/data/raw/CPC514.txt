Course Code: CPC514
Title: CPC 514 Research Methods and Statistics
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/cpc-counselling-psychology-canada/500/cpc-514
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course introduces students to research in the social sciences and provides them with the skills to critically review human wellness literature. Both qualitative and quantitative methodologies will be explored (e.g., autoethnography, indigenous methodologies, experimental design etc.). Proposals from this course may be further developed for later use in thesis and capstone research projects.

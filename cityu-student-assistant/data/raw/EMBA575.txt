Course Code: EMBA575
Title: EMBA 575 Entrepreneurship
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/emba-executive-mba/500/emba-575
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
The entrepreneurship class, EMBA 575, is a comprehensive study of the process of starting and running a successful start-up business. Specific topics covered include entrepreneur leadership tools, creative techniques for idea generation, analytical techniques and tests for new products and services.  Our class addresses a wide range of topics, including the definition and characteristics of entrepreneurship, the generation and evaluation of business ideas, the development of a comprehensive business plan, marketing and sales strategies, financing and funding options, operations and management practices, legal and regulatory issues, and provides real-world cases of successful and unsuccessful start-up businesses.

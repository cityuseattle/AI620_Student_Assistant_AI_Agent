Course Code: EDLD659
Title: EDLD 659 Executive Academy in Transformational Leadership
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/edld-education-leadership/600/edld-659
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: None

Description:
The Executive Academy focuses on the role of the superintendent and executive leader in leading systemic change.  Instructors share how school system leaders promote extraordinary reforms, maintain accountability, and achieve significant improvements in teaching and learning. Candidates create transformational change goals, strategies, and action plans.  They examine the work of superintendents and their leadership teams in fostering long-lasting change in high-performance school systems.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Demonstrate readiness to be an executive leader.
2. Demonstrate understanding of facets of system-wide leadership.
3. Develop a theory of action for system-wide improvement.
4. Demonstrate an ability to diagnose and resolve system conflicts, issues, and problems.

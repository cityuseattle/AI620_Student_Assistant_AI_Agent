Course Code: RESR617
Title: RESR 617 Research Fundamentals
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/resr-research/600/resr-617
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course will review the elements essential to a dissertation or research article. Students will evaluate research articles for design and statistical analyses, as well as summarize and critique resources in their field of study.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Develop an understanding of the culture and process of research.
Critically evaluate purpose, design, data analysis, and findings of research articles.
Summarize and evaluate a collection of literature relevant to a specific research topic.

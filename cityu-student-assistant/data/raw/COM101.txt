Course Code: COM101
Title: COM 101 Speech and Presentation Skills (HU)
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/com-communications-revised/100/com-101
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Public speaking is an introductory course in the theory and practice of speaking in public, with the emphasis on the speaker-audience relationship. In this course, students will learn how to analyze the speaking situation, choose appropriate topics, conduct research, organize ideas and use evidence to support their ideas, deliver speeches effectively, and develop the ability to critically listen.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Apply critical thinking skills to multiple speaking contexts.
2. Assess and apply ethical standards and principles to public speaking contexts.
3. Demonstrate the ability to speak professionally.
4. Demonstrate credibility.
5. Understand the role of culture in public speaking.

Course Code: EMBA600
Title: EMBA 600 Residency II
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/emba-executive-mba/600/emba-600
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This residency course provides students with an opportunity for a five-week immersive experience exploring different business operation strategies and techniques on an international scale. Business professionals from top companies selected by our partner universities in Mexico will share their invaluable insights on changing trends in their fields of expertise. In this course, students will expand their understanding of global management, as well as hone their intercultural management skills, while working on real-world projects with companies in their destination locations.

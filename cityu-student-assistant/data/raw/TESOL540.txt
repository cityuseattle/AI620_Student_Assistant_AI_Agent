Course Code: TESOL540
Title: TESOL 540 Strategies for Teaching the Four Language Skills *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/tesol-teaching-english-to-speakers-of-other-languages/500/tesol-540
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: TESOL 510

Description:
TESOL 540 examines a variety of strategies for teaching reading, writing, listening, and speaking to English language learners. Candidates review fundamental concepts and popular teaching methodologies and gain practical knowledge for teaching these skills. Candidates learn and demonstrate a variety of techniques for teaching the four language skills and design a variety of lessons incorporating these skills.

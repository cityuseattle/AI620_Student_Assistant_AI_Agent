Course Code: ECC509
Title: ECC 509 Preparation for Professional Growth
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ecc-education-core-canada/500/ecc-509
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
As future school leaders, students conduct a self-assessment and a review of their leadership knowledge and skills. Students develop a Professional Growth Plan to explore and refine their leadership qualities, skills, and actions as leaders. Students develop a foundational understanding of leadership and specifically the importance of relational leadership.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Analyze power, structures of inequality, and social systems.
2. Evaluate the professional roles of school leaders.
3. Evaluate the various qualities, attitudes, and actions of leaders.
4. Create a professional self-assessment, growth plan, and emerging leadership philosophy.

Course Code: COUN616
Title: COUN 616 Counseling Older Adults
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/coun-counseling/600/coun-616
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course presents critical information for counselors working with older adults. Current issues and pragmatic considerations, and their implications for treatment will be explored. Students will engage in study and discussion of social, generational, cultural, financial, medical and ethical concerns specific to the older adult population.

Course Code: MBA625
Title: MBA 625 Business Administration Internship *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/mba-master-of-business-administration/600/mba-625
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: Completion of 30 MBA quarter credit hours at City University of Seattle with a cumulative GPA of 3.25.

Description:
This internship course provides students with a business-related learning experience designed to enhance the understanding of business practices within a given field. Students will focus on the development of professional practice and application of theories and concepts. By the end of the course, students will have a deeper understanding of the real life work environment in the field of business administration. Note: Previously named "Sustainable Business Internship".

Outcomes:
None

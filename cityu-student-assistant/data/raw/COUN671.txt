Course Code: COUN671
Title: COUN 671 Capstone I
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/coun-counseling/600/coun-671
Credits: 1
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Prior to completing their program of study, students will submit a capstone project and complete a summative assessment of learning.  This course is designed to assist students in identifying, researching and completing their Capstone Project during their final year in the program while completing their internship placement requirements.  The project will be data driven for all tracks and each student will work directly with their advisor beginning in the final quarter of the program prior to their internship placement beginning.

Outcomes:
None

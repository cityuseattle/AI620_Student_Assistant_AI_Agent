Course Code: LDRD640
Title: LDRD 640 Strategic Thinking
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ldrd-leadership/600/ldrd-640
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course explores how leaders develop strategic thinking in themselves and others. Emphasis is placed on stakeholder input; strategic intent and planning; evaluation of internal and external influences; and managing globally-minded, results-oriented strategic alliances.  Students align strategies to an organization's mission and vision.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Design an execution plan for a strategic initiative.
Perform a strategic perspective analysis.
Create a strategic plan for an organizational initiative.

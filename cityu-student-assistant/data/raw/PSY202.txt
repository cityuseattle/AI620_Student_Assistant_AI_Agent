Course Code: PSY202
Title: PSY 202 Understanding Human Development (SS)
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/psy-psychology/200/psy-202
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: None

Description:
An investigation into the biological, social, and psychological forces that shape the various stages of our lives. The course also examines patterns of difference due to factors such as gender, culture, disability, and socioeconomic status.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Critically apply theories and concepts of lifespan human development into practice.
Apply an understanding of major psychological, social, and biological theories and concepts to human development

Course Code: MBA560
Title: MBA 560 Practical Business Applications I *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/mba-master-of-business-administration/500/mba-560
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: This course is to be taken at the end of the MBA program after all of the required MBA core courses (MBA 500, MBA 501, MBA 511, MBA 515, MBA 520, MBA 525). Students must also have completed MBA 561 or be enrolled concurrently in this course. Exceptions to this policy will require the approval of the MBA Program Director.

Description:
In this course you will prepare for your application project. You will exercise the interviewing, project planning, proposal writing and other skills learned in the program in a real world situation.

Outcomes:
None

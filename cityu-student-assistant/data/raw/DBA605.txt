Course Code: DBA605
Title: DBA 605 Organizational Development Theories and Trends
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/dba-doctor-business-administration/600/dba-605
Credits: 6
Prerequisites: None

Description:
In this course, students analyze leadership theories, business models, and ethical principles associated with organizational development (OD). Topics include organizational ecology, change models, leadership approaches, and ethical frameworks. By completing this course, students develop the requisite skills to assess and improve current practices of an organization relative to its mission and values and investigate the opportunities that lead to organizational change.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Analyze business and organizational development theory, models, and application.
2. Evaluate an organization's structure and operations to identify needs and appropriate interventions for improvement.
3. Analyze leadership and change management theory, models, and application.
4. Demonstrate effective leadership and strategic planning to guide an organization through change.

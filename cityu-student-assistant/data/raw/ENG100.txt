Course Code: ENG100
Title: ENG 100 College Reading/Writing (HU)
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/eng-english/100/eng-100
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: None

Description:
In this course, students learn the foundations of academic reading and writing. The course gives a comprehensive review of standard English grammar, punctuation, and sentence structure. Students practice developing appropriate language and organization for academic assignments and apply APA writing style. This course also emphasizes steps in the writing process, which allows students to become successful in the academic learning environment.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Apply reading strategies to understand key points, purpose, perspectives, and organization in various texts.
2. Employ Standard English grammar, punctuation, and APA style references.
3. Demonstrate understanding of common academic rhetorical patterns (narrative, summary, process, cause/effect, compare/contrast).
4. Create well-developed academic paragraphs with main and subordinate ideas.
5. Develop a multi-paragraph final expository essay.

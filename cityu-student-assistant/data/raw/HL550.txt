Course Code: HL550
Title: HL 550 Healthcare Policy and Economics
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/hl-healthcare/500/hl-550
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
In this course, students examine the application of health policy and economic principles in regard to managerial decision making. Students learn about the process for policy development and implementation, key stakeholders and interest groups involved in the health policy process, and how health policy changes over time within the United States. Students assess and discuss key policy initiatives related to cost, quality, and access.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Analyze the application of healthcare policy and economic principles in regard to managerial decision making.
Assess and explain key healthcare policy initiatives related to cost, quality, and access.
Differentiate the roles and responsibilities of key stakeholders and interest groups involved in the healthcare policy process.
Examine how healthcare policy changes over time within the United States.
Explain the process for healthcare policy development and implementation.

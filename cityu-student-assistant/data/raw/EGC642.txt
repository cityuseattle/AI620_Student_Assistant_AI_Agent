Course Code: EGC642
Title: EGC 642 Pro Cert Entry Seminar *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/egc-professional-school-counseling/600/egc-642
Credits: 1
Prerequisites: Completed application packet with required forms per Guidance & Counseling Program; sent to Director for review

Description:
The ProCert Entry Seminar is based on the requirements of the Professional Educator Standards Board (PESB). Entry Seminar tasks will include the completion of the professional audit, creation of a professional growth plan, identification of a professional growth team (PGT) and a thorough overview of the expectations of school counselor competencies at the professional, as described by PESB.

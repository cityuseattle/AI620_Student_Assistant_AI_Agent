Course Code: HUM200
Title: HUM 200 Introduction to Philosophy (HU)
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/hum-humanities/200/hum-200
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course is an overview of classical and contemporary philosophical theories. Students will explore philosophy and its practical applications to current issues. This course will address theories of knowledge, ethics, political and social philosophy, as well as the philosophies of religion, the mind, and science.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Analyze classical and contemporary theories in philosophy.
Apply philosophical concepts to contemporary events.
Assess the purpose of philosophy and the value of philosophical inquiry.

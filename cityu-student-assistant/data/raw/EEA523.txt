Course Code: EEA523
Title: EEA 523 Teaching, Learning, and Assessment
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/eea-educational-administration/500/eea-523
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
In this course, candidates learn to design, implement, assess, and evaluate student learning and teacher pedagogy. Candidates learn about state and national professional teaching standards and pedagogical assessments to evaluate and guide teachers along the career continuum:  pre-service, beginning, teacher assistance programs, professional certification, National Board Teacher Certification, and career-long professional development.  Candidates build capacity to make a positive impact on student learning and high-quality teaching in schools.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Evaluate curriculum and instructional models and assessment practices that improve instruction.
2. Evaluate instructional pedagogy utilizing evidence and field-based educational research.
3. Train teachers in the examination of student work and the use of protocols for professional collaboration, through mentoring, coaching, and clinical supervision.

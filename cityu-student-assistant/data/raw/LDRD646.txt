Course Code: LDRD646
Title: LDRD 646 Developing Leaders of the Future
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ldrd-leadership/600/ldrd-646
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course focuses on developing ethical leaders of the future. Emphasis is placed on ethics, accountability, and empowerment through interdependent relationships with stakeholders. Students will be equipped to develop leaders of the future who can incorporate key initiatives into organizational strategies.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Synthesize a vision for developing leaders based on an organization's key initiatives and organizational strategies.
Analyze the status of personal leadership development.
Prepare an action plan for strategically developing future leaders.

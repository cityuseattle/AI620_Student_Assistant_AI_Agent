Course Code: LDRD600
Title: LDRD 600 Introduction to Doctoral Program
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ldrd-leadership/600/ldrd-600
Credits: 1
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Students will become familiar with the CityU Doctoral Program, online success tips, and academic writing resources. Tutorials and short quizzes or exercises in scholastic honesty, writing conventions, and APA citations will help prepare students for doctoral-level work. This is a self-paced class.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Demonstrate an understanding of the doctoral program and its requirements.
Understand the basics of academic writing and APA.
Show the ability to use the learning management system.

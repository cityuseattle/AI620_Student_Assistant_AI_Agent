Course Code: ENT620
Title: ENT 620 Economics of Entrepreneurship
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ent-entrepreneurship/600/ent-620
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Financial knowledge is the biggest predictor of success or failure of a business. This course analyzes the unique financial issues facing entrepreneurial firms. Topics include assessing financial performance, financial forecasting and planning, financial management of rapidly growing businesses, start-up ventures, valuation, sources of financing, and daily accounting issues.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Develop a forecast of capital needs.
2. Evaluate cash basis and accrual accounting to determine the impact of cash budgeting on a start-up.
3. Assess financial forecasting and planning.
4. Contrast different types of financing including bank loans, SBA loans, venture capital, angel investing, and crowdfunding.

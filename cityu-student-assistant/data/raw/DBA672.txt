Course Code: DBA672
Title: DBA 672 Doctoral Seminar II
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/dba-doctor-business-administration/600/dba-672
Credits: 2
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Doctoral Seminar II is the second of three courses in the Doctoral Seminar series. In the Doctoral Seminar II course, students continue working on their Applied Business Research Project proposal. The Applied Business Research Project proposal complements their specialized study and dissertation topic. This second course of the series focuses on developing arguments that support researchable topics and writing the components of the Applied Business Research Project proposal. Upon completion of this course, students will have formalized the proposal for their Applied Business Research Project.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Develop arguments that support researchable topics.
2. Build connections between the Applied Business Research Project and the student's dissertation.
3. Complete the Applied Business Research Project proposal.

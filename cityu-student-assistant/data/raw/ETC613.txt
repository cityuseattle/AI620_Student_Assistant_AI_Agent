Course Code: ETC613
Title: ETC 613 Literacy II: Instruction and Assessment *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/etc-teacher-certification/600/etc-613
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: Admission to M.Ed Program, Completion of ETC 612

Description:
This course is the second of three courses in the literacy sequence for the graduate Teacher Certification Program which examines a variety of assessment and instructional strategies. Topics include reading comprehension strategies, informational text, complex text, text density, writing and alignment with State standards. Upon completion of this course, candidates are able to effectively assess and teach literacy skills for K-8 students.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Utilize assessment strategies.
Utilize strategies to read and comprehend complex literary and informational text.
Select and implement appropriate assessment strategies.
Select and implement strategies to guide students to read and comprehend complex literary and informational text.
Demonstrate strategies to guide students to read with sufficient accuracy and fluency to support comprehension.
Design and implement engaging learning experiences.

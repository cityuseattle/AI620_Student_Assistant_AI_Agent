Course Code: TESOL525
Title: TESOL 525 Methods of Teaching Language *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/tesol-teaching-english-to-speakers-of-other-languages/500/tesol-525
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: TESOL 510

Description:
TESOL 525 introduces candidates to a historical overview of language teaching methodology spanning the 19th century to present day. Candidates examine and analyze a variety of teaching methods and approaches that reflect language learning theories and schools of thought. During this course, candidates gain a fuller understanding of the principles underlying the choices teachers make regarding classroom practices. Candidates also complete a minimum of eight classroom observation hours and analyze and reflect on this experience.

Course Code: ECU535
Title: ECU 535 Multicultural Perspectives: Increasing Family/Community Engagement and Student Achievement *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ecu-education-core-graduate/500/ecu-535
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: This course is open to graduate-level candidates in education programs.

Description:
This course provides candidates with the skills, perspectives and strategies to engage family and community members for the purpose of increasing P-12 student academic achievement. Candidates collaborate with a variety of individuals and organizations to deepen understanding of access and barriers for parents and their children. Through the development of a proposed Family/Community Engagement Plan, candidates will apply skills and strategies that are consistent with cultural competence, academic inquiry and P-12 school improvement goals.

Course Code: HUM220
Title: HUM 220 Introduction to the Humanities (HU)
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/hum-humanities/200/hum-220
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course is an introduction to college-level studies in the humanities. The course is a multi-perspective exploration of systems of thought, ideas, and creations - including arts, music, literature, and architecture - of Western and other cultures. Through reflection and analysis, students will develop an understanding of the place of arts in their daily lives.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Connect historical, social, and cultural influences to interpret a work of art.
Explain how fine arts are expressions of diverse human experiences and approaches.
Compare and contrast the meaning of major works of art from both Western and non-Western cultures.

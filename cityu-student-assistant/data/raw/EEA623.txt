Course Code: EEA623
Title: EEA 623 Field Experience in Educational Leadership
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/eea-educational-administration/600/eea-623
Credits: 1 TO 6
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This field experience is a continuation of an internship that engages the intern in demonstrating the entry level knowledge, skills and dispositions/cultural competence of the P-12 principal or program administrator as verified by mentor and field supervisor. Capacity for leadership is documented in a certification portfolio. Successful completion of the internship partially completes requirements for residency certification as a principal or program administrator.

Outcomes:
None

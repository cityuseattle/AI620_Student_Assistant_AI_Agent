Course Code: BAM400
Title: BAM 400 Organizational Communications (HU)
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/bam-bachelor-of-arts-management/400/bam-400
Credits: 15
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Students will analyze and practice the most important types of organizational communications used in today’s companies. Critical thinking skills will include building and analyzing arguments, working with forms and standards of critical thinking, and evaluating sources of information in the context of business. Students will also examine ethical decision-making and practical applications involving ethical problems that arise in business. Students will practice effective articulation and communication of solutions, including assessing their management communication style and identifying areas for improvement.

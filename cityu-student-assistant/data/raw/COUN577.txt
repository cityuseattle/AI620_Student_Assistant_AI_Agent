Course Code: COUN577
Title: COUN 577 Practicum II
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/coun-counseling/500/coun-577
Credits: 2
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This is the second course of a two-part practicum sequence (COUN 575, 577). For the entire Practicum sequence, students complete 100 total hours and 40 direct hours of client contact at a program-approved site. Students will meet weekly with a site supervisor for individual/triadic supervision, and weekly with the course instructor for group supervision. The student will carry professional liability insurance and abide by the ACA Code of Ethics. Students are expected to use and complete the forms included in the MAC Clinical Training Manual. Students are expected to use and complete the forms included in the Training Manual, and students are responsible for uploading required documents and information into Tevera.  Grading for this course is Pass/Fail. To pass, students must meet minimum performance requirements and submit all assignments by the due date.

Course Code: LDRD630
Title: LDRD 630 Ethical Organizational Leadership
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ldrd-leadership/600/ldrd-630
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course explores complex social, political, and global ethical challenges facing organizational leaders. Emphasis is placed on the implementation of organizational strategy founded in a value-based mission. Students will develop and communicate ethical leadership plans for organizational stakeholders to apply to everyday decisions.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Examine the ethical aspects of social, political, and cultural influences that impact global organizations.
Apply ethical frameworks to decision making.
Demonstrate understanding of ethical perspectives and components of ethical leadership.

Course Code: EGC528
Title: EGC 528 Issues in Social Emotional Learning
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/egc-professional-school-counseling/500/egc-528
Credits: 2
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course examines current issues related to social emotional learning in schools and the most recent legislation surrounding it. This course will examine Social Emotional Learning curriculums and programs through an equity and anti-racist lens to ensure critical evaluation, reflection, and equitable practices during their implementation with children.

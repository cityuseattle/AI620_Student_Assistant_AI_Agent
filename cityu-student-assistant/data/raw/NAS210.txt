Course Code: NAS210
Title: NAS 210 Ecological Science Lab (NS)
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/nas-natural-science/200/nas-210
Credits: 1
Prerequisites: None

Description:
The purpose of this course is for the teacher candidates to understand the relationship of ecological science and scientific inquiry. Candidates develop an ecological science lab experiment using the scientific inquiry method. Candidates demonstrate their understanding of the procedures of conducting simple investigations and the relationship between science, ecology and sustainability.

None

Course Code: PSY422
Title: PSY 422 Child Psychology (SS)
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/psy-psychology/400/psy-422
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course discusses the issues of children's biological and psychosocial development, relationship, and culture in a social context. It addresses topics such as physical, cognitive, social, and intellectual growth, language acquisition, family and peers, popularity, impact of mass media and culture on child's development.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Assess the relationship between heredity and environment in human development.
Discuss the role of language acquisition in child and pre-adolescent development.
Discuss moral and social development of children and pre-adolescents.
Describe various perspectives of cognitive development of children and pre-adolescents.
Trace the physical, cognitive, and social development of children and pre-adolescents.
Compare and contrast various theories of child and pre-adolescent development.

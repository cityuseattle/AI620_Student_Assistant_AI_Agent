Course Code: PM480
Title: PM 480 Project Integration Action Learning Project *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/pm-project-management/400/pm-480
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: PM 401, PM 409, PM 410, PM 404, PM 406, PM 408, PM 413, PM 414

Description:
This capstone course is a cumulative assessment of project management knowledge areas, skills, tools, and technology. The student will construct elements of a Project Management Notebook (PMNB/e-portfolio) as a final capstone project.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Construct and demonstrate communication practices to stakeholders.
Apply project management fundamentals to differing industries.
Design lessons learned into lifelong learning practices.
Evaluate the implications of project management to organizational effectiveness.
Acquire a foundation of fundamental project management knowledge areas via the Project Management Body of Knowledge (PMBOK® Guide).

Course Code: CPC527
Title: CPC 527 Group Counselling Psychology
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/cpc-counselling-psychology-canada/500/cpc-527
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course provides an experiential introduction to group processing theory including exposure to a variety of therapeutic groups designed to provide support for deeper exploration of self and relationship to others. Students will participate both as facilitators and members of a variety of therapeutic groups. A number of topics will be explored throughout the course including: 1) the history of group therapy; 2) the benefits and challenges of therapeutic groups; and 3) the influences of culture, class, gender, sexuality, and age on group dynamics.

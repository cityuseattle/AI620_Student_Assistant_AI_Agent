Course Code: DBA671
Title: DBA 671 Doctoral Seminar I
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/dba-doctor-business-administration/600/dba-671
Credits: 2
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Doctoral Seminar I is the first of three courses in the Doctoral Seminar series. In the Doctoral Seminar series, students plan, conduct, and complete an Applied Business Research Project that complements their dissertation topic. Emphasis is placed on connecting coursework, research, and applying communication strategies to the dissertation and dissemination of findings. Students examine program expectations and dissertation milestones to develop a roadmap to complete their doctoral journey. Students analyze the connections between coursework, research, and dissemination, and draft an outline of the student’s Applied Business Research Project. Upon completion of this course, students will have completed the initial components of their Applied Business Research Project proposal.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Examine program expectations and dissertation milestones to complete the doctoral journey.
2. Analyze connections between coursework, research, and dissemination.
3. Draft a plan to complete an Applied Business Research Project.

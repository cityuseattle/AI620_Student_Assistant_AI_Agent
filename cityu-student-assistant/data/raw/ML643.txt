Course Code: ML643
Title: ML 643 Applied Research Capstone *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ml-managerial-leadership/600/ml-643
Credits: 6
Prerequisites: All core courses must be completed prior to enrollment.

Description:
This is the final capstone course for the Master of Science in Management and Leadership, the Master of Science in Management, the Master of Science in HR Management, and the Master of Science in Organizational Leadership. Students will create a capstone project that includes a personal leadership development plan and a research report that addresses a current problem in their field.

Outcomes:
This course will enable students to demonstrate mastery of their program outcomes as listed on the program page in this catalog.

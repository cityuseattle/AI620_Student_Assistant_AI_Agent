Course Code: EEA622
Title: EEA 622 Internship III *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/eea-educational-administration/600/eea-622
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Internship III engages the intern in demonstrating the entry level knowledge, skills and dispositions/cultural competence of the P-12 principal and program administrator as verified by mentor and field supervisor. Capacity for leadership is documented in a certification portfolio. Successful completion of the internship and its documentation partially completes requirements for residency certification as a principal or program administrator.

Outcomes:
None

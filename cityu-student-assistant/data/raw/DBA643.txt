Course Code: DBA643
Title: DBA 643 Qualitative Research Methods for Business Administration
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/dba-doctor-business-administration/600/dba-643
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Several types of qualitative methods of data collection will be covered in this course, including various types of interviews, content analysis and case studies.  Students will be able to evaluate different methodologies for validity and reliability as well as to determine which is/are most appropriate for different types of research questions.

Outcomes:
As a result of this course, students will know or be able to do the following:
1. Analyze qualitative data and create a research report from the data.
2. Evaluate different qualitative strategies examining underpinnings, data collection methods, and methods of analysis.
3. Generate a research proposal by aligning the topic to research questions then the research questions to the appropriate methodological strategy.

Course Code: COUN687
Title: COUN 687 Treatment of Addictions: Individual, Group, and Family Addictions Counseling using ASAM Criteria *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/coun-counseling/600/coun-687
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: COUN 680; COUN 684

Description:
Using evidence-based practice, students develop conceptual knowledge and practical skills to diagnose and treat substance abuse disorders. This course covers screening, assessment, and diagnoses, as well as determining American Society of Addiction Medicine (ASAM) placement criteria and level of care. Students learn the appropriate treatment modalities for individuals, groups, and families. Co-occurring disorders and trauma are also addressed. Students will receive a packet of materials from the instructor at the beginning of the course.

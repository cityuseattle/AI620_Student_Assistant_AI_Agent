Course Code: CPC512
Title: CPC 512 Family Systems Therapy
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/cpc-counselling-psychology-canada/500/cpc-512
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course introduces students to classic and contemporary family theories and their clinical applications. Students will explore the history and evolution of family therapy with a special focus on diversity and the sociocultural context(s) within which families are embedded. A wide range of traditional and non-traditional family models will be examined (e. g., nuclear families, blended families, LGBT families etc.), including students' own family histories and their impact on students' development.

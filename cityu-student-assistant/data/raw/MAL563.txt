Course Code: MAL563
Title: MAL 563 Philanthropy and Strategic Fund Development
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/mal-management-leadership/500/mal-563
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course emphasizes the changing expectations for ethical behavior and accountability that currently impact the practice of philanthropy. Fundraising theory is applied to the development of a comprehensive program, personal fundraising philosophy, and framework. Additionally, aspects of the fundraising process for nonprofit organizations will be examined including general fundraising principles, fundraising techniques, sources of donations, and key aspects of developing and managing the strategic fundraising process.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Analyze a nonprofit organization’s fund development plan in light of overall mission.
Develop a comprehensive fund development plan for a nonprofit organization.
Examine the resources, strategies, and best practices used in nonprofit fund development.

Course Code: ECC540
Title: ECC 540 Foundations of Coaching
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ecc-education-core-canada/500/ecc-540
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: None

Description:
The ability to develop a coach approach is an essential skill for future leaders. Students develop their understanding of the foundations of coaching including the principles of coaching, coaching core competencies, and coaching modalities. Students will have guided practice in utilizing a model for guiding coaching conversations that has been successfully used in a variety of areas of leadership.

Outcomes:
1. Demonstrate professional competency in the use of coaching conversations as an effective leadership tool.
2. Apply skills at using a recognized coaching approach.
3. Examine the principles, competencies, modalities, and practices of coaching.
4. Evaluate the various approaches to coaching and how they can be integrated.
5. Design specific coaching conversations that can be utilized to promote skill development, resolve conflict, and achieve desired goals.
6. Formulate opportunities for personal and professional enrichment through guided practice and feedback.

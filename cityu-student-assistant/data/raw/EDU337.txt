Course Code: EDU337
Title: EDU 337 Practicum *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/edu-education/300/edu-337
Credits: 1
Prerequisites: Admission to BA in Education Program or Endorsement Program

Description:
This course covers effective integration of instructional strategies and assessments of a specific endorsement content area. This course provides the field experience required to meet state clinical requirements.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Demonstrate skill in assessment of student learning.
2. Exhibit essential dispositions of a professional educator.
3. Involve families and communities in student learning.
4. Demonstrate skill in engaging students in meaningful learning activities.
5. Demonstrate skill in planning for instruction.
6. Demonstrate positive impact on student learning.

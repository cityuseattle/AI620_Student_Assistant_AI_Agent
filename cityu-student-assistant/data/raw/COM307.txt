Course Code: COM307
Title: COM 307 Critical Thinking in Communications (HU)
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/com-communications-revised/300/com-307
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course critically analyzes communication theories as a foundation for framing communication practices and strategies used in various communication settings. Key themes in the field of communications, the role of technology in communication theories, and application of theory to personal, social, and professional interaction will be critically explored.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Critically analyze, assess, and compare a range of communication theories.
2. Apply communication theories to current events, real-life situations, and first-hand experience.
3. Demonstrate the ability to communicate professionally.
4. Assess and critically analyze ethical standards and principles as they apply to a range of communication theories.
5. Identify and explain major communication theories.

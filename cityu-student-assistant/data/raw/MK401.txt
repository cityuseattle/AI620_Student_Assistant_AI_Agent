Course Code: MK401
Title: MK 401 E-Marketing *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/mk-marketing/400/mk-401
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: MK 300 or its equivalent.

Description:
This course examines e-marketing's various roles in an organization's total marketing program. Topics include target audience analysis, developing a site plan, creating a content development strategy, email marketing campaigns, search engine optimization, co-branding and community strategy, customer service, ad buys, sales/lead conversion, using site metrics, and ethical considerations. Students will plan & develop e-marketing strategies and a campaign for a real or imaginary small business.

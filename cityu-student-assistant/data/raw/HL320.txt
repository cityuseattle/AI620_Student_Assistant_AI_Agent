Course Code: HL320
Title: HL 320 Issues and Trends in Healthcare Administration
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/hl-healthcare/300/hl-320
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course covers changes and trends in the American healthcare system, including the increased focus on long-term care, and the increasing shortage of healthcare professionals. Emphasis is placed on healthcare equity and access, the rising cost of medical care and prescription drugs, and technological advances.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Examine and assess equity in terms of healthcare.
Outline the factors that affect access to healthcare in the United States.
Appraise the challenges facing the long-term care industry.
Evaluate the trend and impact of the rising cost of healthcare and prescription drugs.
Evaluate trends in the use of technological advances in healthcare.

Course Code: ECC601
Title: ECC 601 Counselling Field Experience
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ecc-education-core-canada/600/ecc-601
Credits: 1
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Students are oriented to Internship and the value and the purpose of the internship courses and experience. Learners practice basic counselling microskills, confidentiality, reflective practice, exploring positionality, and exploring barriers to engagement within counselling.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Explore microskills in practice.
2. Explore obligations of confidentiality.
3. Use professional terms.
4. Apply a reflective process.
5. Explore one's own positionality.
6. Identify and respond to barriers to engagement.

Course Code: MFT504
Title: MFT 504 Family of Origin from Systemic Perspective
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/mft-marriage-and-family-therapy/500/mft-504
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Students in the Family of Origin from a Systemic Perspective course will explore the foundational concepts of family systems theory with an emphasis on multigenerational dynamics and their influence on individual development. Students will critically examine their own family histories and socio-cultural roots, gaining insights into how these elements shape personal development and professional perspectives. Through the integration of multicultural frameworks, students will enhance their cultural competence and humility in counseling, fostering an understanding of diverse familial and developmental contexts.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Understand the fundamental concepts of family systems theory, focusing on multigenerational dynamics and their impact on individual development.
2. Critically examine their own family history and socio-cultural roots, identifying how these factors have shaped their personal development and current perspectives.
3. Apply multicultural perspectives to understand diverse familial and developmental contexts, enhancing their cultural competence and humility in counseling.
4. Engage in experiential learning activities that foster creative exploration of family dynamics and personal history, promoting deeper self-awareness and professional growth.

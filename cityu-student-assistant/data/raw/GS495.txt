Course Code: GS495
Title: GS 495 General Studies Degree Capstone *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/gs-general-studies/400/gs-495
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: Completion of all other courses in student's program, or program director approval.

Description:
GS 495 is the final course in the General Studies Bachelor of Science program. In this capstone course, students complete assignments, including a final portfolio integrating previous work and synthesizing their learning with an emphasis on their professional development goals. Students should complete all other courses in their program before registering for this course or have program director approval.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
PLO2: Demonstrate active collaboration with others to develop concise, logical, and consistent communications.
PLO3: Employ research strategies and critical thinking skills to determine a logical sequence of events.
PLO5: Model diverse perspectives in a global context.
PLO4: Extend ethical perspectives to sustain organizational values.
PLO6: Interpret new learning and insights to develop knowledge, skills, and abilities.
PLO1: Apply academic learning and professional experience to broaden personal perspectives.

Course Code: ETC608
Title: ETC 608 Practicum
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/etc-teacher-certification/600/etc-608
Credits: 1
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course covers effective integration, instructional strategies and assessments of the specific endorsement content area. This course provides the field experience required to meet state clinical requirements.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Demonstrate skill in assessment of student learning.
Exhibit essential dispositions of a professional educator.
Involve families and communities in student learning.
Demonstrate skill in engaging students in meaningful learning activities.
Demonstrate skill in planning for instruction.
Demonstrate positive impact on student learning.

Course Code: DBA630
Title: DBA 630 Business Process Analysis and Strategic Management
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/dba-doctor-business-administration/600/dba-630
Credits: 6
Prerequisites: None

Description:
In this course, students examine an organization’s operational processes that inform short- and long-term strategic planning. Students investigate forward-looking ethical strategies that are socially as well as environmentally responsible. Focusing on organizational policies and the organization’s supply chain, students develop the requisite knowledge to become ethical leaders who integrate social, economic, and environmental values into responsible business strategies.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Analyze and apply ethical decision making in domestic and global contexts.
2. Analyze models and theories of corporate social responsibility (CSR) in domestic and global contexts.
3. Evaluate an organization's social, economic and environmental impact on domestic and global scales.
4. Create strategic recommendations for improving an organization's social, economic, and/or environmental responsibility.

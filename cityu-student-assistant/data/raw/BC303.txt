Course Code: BC303
Title: BC 303 Statistics (NS)
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/bc-business-core/300/bc-303
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: None

Description:
BC303, Statistics focuses on the usefulness and limitations of statistical processes and their present day applications. Topics include: descriptive statistics, probability theory and distributions, sampling, hypothesis testing, regression, and correlation models. Students will focus on problem formulation, technique selection and results interpretation.

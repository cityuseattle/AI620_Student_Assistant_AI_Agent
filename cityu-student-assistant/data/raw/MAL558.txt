Course Code: MAL558
Title: MAL 558 High-Performing Global Teams
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/mal-management-leadership/500/mal-558
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Leaders require skills to effectively collaborate within a multi-cultural environment, evaluate culturally diverse talent, communicate team goals, and foster global team interactions. To compete globally, leaders need to learn practical leadership strategies for building cohesive, motivated, high-performing, global teams. Students will explore team-building strategies effective in virtual and global environments.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Understanding differences between individual competencies and the needs of globally diverse teams.
Analyze leadership strategies that develop global team behavior.
Examine global diversity and identify leadership strategies that deliver high-performing diverse team results.

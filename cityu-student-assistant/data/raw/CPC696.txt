Title: CPC 696 Counselling Psychology: Thesis *

Credits: 5
Prerequisites: CPC 514, CPC 520

Description:
This course guides the student through the steps of producing a major scholarly document. The product of the course is a thesis, which is the culmination of a student's learning in the Counselling Psychology program.

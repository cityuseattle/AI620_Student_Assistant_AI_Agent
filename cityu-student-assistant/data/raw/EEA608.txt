Course Code: EEA608
Title: EEA 608 Reflective Seminar
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/eea-educational-administration/600/eea-608
Credits: 1
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This reflective seminar engages the intern in applying and practicing the entry level knowledge, skills, dispositions/cultural competence, roles and responsibilities of the P-12 principal and program administrator. This seminar requires the intern to demonstrate that she/he meets the residency-level standards for (ISLLC-based) Washington State certification as a principal or program administrator through reflective practice, and data driven personal goals. In a supportive setting, the intern shares evidence of her/his skills as an educational leader, provides feedback to other interns, and shares internship activities and experiences.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Facilitate collaborative partnerships.
2. Develop leadership capacity.
3. Ensure a school environment conducive to student learning.
4. Influence political and social environments.
5. Manage instructional supervision processes.
6. Exhibit leadership integrity.

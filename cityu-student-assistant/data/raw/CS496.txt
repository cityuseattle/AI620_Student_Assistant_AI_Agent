Course Code: CS496
Title: CS 496 Capstone II - Project Implementation and Validation *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/cs-computer-science/400/cs-496
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: CS 495

Description:
The Capstone Project is a multi-disciplinary team project where the students have the opportunity to apply the concepts learned in their college experience to a real-world problem. In the second course of the sequence, students will implement the project they have identified and designed in the first course, validate their implementation, and present the results of their efforts.

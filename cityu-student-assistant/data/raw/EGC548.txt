Course Code: EGC548
Title: EGC 548 Social and Emotional Considerations for Highly Capable Students
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/egc-professional-school-counseling/500/egc-548
Credits: 2
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course is designed for professionals working with highly capable students in school settings. It will explore current research and materials relevant to the unique social and emotional issues that may arise for gifted and talented students, and provdie opportunitites to explore concrete teaching and counseling strategies that can help this special population of students thrive in the school setting.

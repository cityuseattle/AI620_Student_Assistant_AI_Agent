Course Code: MK388
Title: MK 388 Global Marketing *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/mk-marketing/300/mk-388
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: MK 300 or its equivalent.

Description:
This course provides a basis for examining global marketing opportunities and development of appropriate strategies. Emphasis is placed on environmental and cultural considerations as they impact various elements of the marketing mix. Students will apply these concepts via a market audit and competitive market analysis.

Course Code: ECC515
Title: ECC 515 Leadership and Counselling Diverse Cultures in Education
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ecc-education-core-canada/500/ecc-515
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Students explore the implications of cultural and diversity issues in the school environment. Students build their cultural proficiency, as well as deepen their self-awareness regarding their own culture and world view. Students apply learned cultural and diversity strategies to promote the emotional and physical well-being of all students and improve the school’s learning climate.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Demonstrate an understanding of cultural competence and professional practice.
2. Apply informed awareness of the multiple dimensions of culture in their region.
3. Examine the role of school leaders and their influence on the educational system.
4. Evaluate the many dimensions of culture and the influence of bias, into one's own worldview.
5. Appraise their social responsibility as professionals and citizens.

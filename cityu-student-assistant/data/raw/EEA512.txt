Course Code: EEA512
Title: EEA 512 Academic Inquiry and Action Research
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/eea-educational-administration/500/eea-512
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course introduces the research process including practical action research.  Topics include reflection, practice, analysis and evaluation of information from references and formal published research, and examination of the principles of action research to determine how educators can effectively apply them. Using technology, candidates evaluate research and conduct a formal review of the literature on a selected research topic related to making a positive impact on student learning.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Synthesize a review of current literature on a topic.
2. Analyze and evaluate research articles.
3. Examine action research processes.

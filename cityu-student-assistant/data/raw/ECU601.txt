Course Code: ECU601
Title: ECU 601 Master of Education Capstone Project *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ecu-education-core-graduate/600/ecu-601
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: Admission to the M.Ed. program, completion of ECU 504

Description:
Candidates complete a capstone project centered upon an important and authentic educational problem of practice.  There is an emphasis on the selection and synthesis of existing research into a literature review, as well as the practical application of new knowledge and skills in a classroom setting to improve educational practice.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Describe a problem of practice within a school or classroom setting.
2. Write a research question(s) of appropriate scope to explore a problem of practice within a school or classroom setting.
3. Synthesize existing research into a literature review.
4. Determine and describe appropriate research design, methodology, and methods to effectively explore research question(s).
5. Interpret and analyze data and/or research.
6. Make recommendations for the problem of practice that are connected to research and data.

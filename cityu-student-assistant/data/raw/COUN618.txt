Course Code: COUN618
Title: COUN 618 Human Sexuality
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/coun-counseling/600/coun-618
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course involves the study of human sexuality (e.g., gender, sexual functioning, sexual orientation) and current sexual behaviors and disorders addressed in clinical practice. Topics include sexual development, sexual orientation, sex addiction, addiction to internet pornography, and cybersex. The impact of these behaviors and disorders on intimate relationships and models of clinical interventions will be examined.

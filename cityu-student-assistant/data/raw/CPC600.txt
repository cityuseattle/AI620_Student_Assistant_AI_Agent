Course Code: CPC600
Title: CPC 600 Child and Adolescent Counselling
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/cpc-counselling-psychology-canada/600/cpc-600
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
In this course, students will critically examine a range of therapeutic approaches to working with children and youth. Issues such as sexual identity, mental health, substance use, and peer relations will be explored from a range of theoretical perspectives.

None

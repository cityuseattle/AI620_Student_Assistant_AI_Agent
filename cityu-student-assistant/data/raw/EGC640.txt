Course Code: EGC640
Title: EGC 640 Project in School Counselling and Presentation *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/egc-professional-school-counseling/600/egc-640
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: ECC 511

Description:
Students engage in research to study a problem in relation to school-based counselling. Students prepare a Capstone Project that explores a relevant research question, is based on current literature, and proposes practical recommendations for stakeholders or working practices within the school setting.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Engage in research and demonstrate research skills.
2. Identify a research topic and develop a research question.
3. Analyze and synthesize scholarly literature to guide research/intervention planning.
4. Propose and develop an appropriate methodology, recommendations, or intervention for a real-world problem.
5. Author a graduate-level scholarly project.
6. Demonstrate competency in project management.

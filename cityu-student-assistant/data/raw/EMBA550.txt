Course Code: EMBA550
Title: EMBA 550 Residency I
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/emba-executive-mba/500/emba-550
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This residency course allows students to expand their knowledge of business concepts and strategies by applying them to real-world problems in the context of our complex global economy. Students will, through presentations with practitioner faculty and industry leaders, explore business opportunities and risks through the prism of cross-cultural perspectives. The course is designed to complement other courses within the executive MBA program with more in-depth insights into global industry trends and best practices, as well as continued development of strategic thinking, communication, entrepreneurship, and leadership skills.

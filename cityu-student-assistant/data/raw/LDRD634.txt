Course Code: LDRD634
Title: LDRD 634 Leading in a Global Environment
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ldrd-leadership/600/ldrd-634
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course explores what it takes to become a global leader and why cultural adaptability is important. Emphasis is placed on developing as global leaders and managers; leveraging workplace diversity; optimizing cultural intelligence and adaptability; and working across cultures. Students examine opportunities for development as global leaders and explore cultural intelligence and cultural adaptability in the context of leading in a global environment.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Create a customized plan for developing competencies needed as a global leader.
Evaluate cultural intelligence and adaptability in the context of leading in a global environment.

Course Code: DBA673
Title: DBA 673 Doctoral Seminar III
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/dba-doctor-business-administration/600/dba-673
Credits: 2
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Doctoral Seminar III is the third of three courses in the Doctoral Seminar series. In the Doctoral Seminar III course, students complete an Applied Business Research Project that complements their specialized study and dissertation topic. Emphasis is placed on connecting coursework, research, and applying communication strategies to the dissertation and dissemination of findings. This third course of the series focuses on completing the Applied Business Research Project and developing connections to the dissertation. Upon completing this series of courses, students will have completed and defended their Applied Business Research Project.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Produce a completed Applied Business Research Project.
2. Establish connections between the Applied Business Research Project and the student’s dissertation.
3. Defend arguments in the Applied Business Research Project.

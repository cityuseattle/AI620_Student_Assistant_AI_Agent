Course Code: EMBA580
Title: EMBA 580 Strategic Marketing
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/emba-executive-mba/500/emba-580
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Our class on strategic marketing, EMBA 580, covers the concepts and principles of developing and implementing effective marketing strategies. The strategic marketing class is designed to equip students with the expertise necessary to develop and implement effective marketing strategies. This course covers key topics in marketing, including marketing research, consumer behavior, product development, pricing strategy, promotion and advertising, distribution and channel management, and marketing metrics.

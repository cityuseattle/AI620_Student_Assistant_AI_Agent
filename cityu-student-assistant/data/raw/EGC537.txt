Course Code: EGC537
Title: EGC 537 Strategies for Teaching and Facilitation of Social Emotional Learning
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/egc-professional-school-counseling/500/egc-537
Credits: 2
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course provides teachers and other school professionals with topics and teaching strategies to promote social emotional learning in children and adolescents in school-based settings. The course will explore contributing actors and how teachers and caring professionals can help children and adolescents harness their own potential and to develop their capacity for having fulfilled interpersonal interactinos, empathy, identity development, and a positive sense of self.

Course Code: TESOL515
Title: TESOL 515 TESOL Essentials
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/tesol-teaching-english-to-speakers-of-other-languages/500/tesol-515
Credits: 6
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course is an introductory look into the essential principles, methods, and vital concepts that comprise the basis of English language teaching pedagogy. TESOL Essentials course topics include second language acquisition theory, teaching strategies for the five skills (Reading, Writing, Speaking, Listening, and Grammar), materials and assessment development, and classroom management. After completing this course, students will have gained the skills and knowledge necessary to begin teaching abroad in their designated TESOL placement.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Apply established language theory and philosophy used in the modern English Language Learner (ELL) classroom.
2. Analyze how students develop language skills in reading, writing, listening, speaking, and grammar.
3. Develop lesson plans, materials, and assessments that can be applied to young learners in the field.
4. Deploy effective feedback strategies that facilitate academic growth and achievement of the learner.
5. Manage classrooms in a way that facilitates engagement, collaboration, and cooperation among students and educators.

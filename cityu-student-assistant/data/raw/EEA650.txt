Course Code: EEA650
Title: EEA 650 Project and Portfolio Presentation *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/eea-educational-administration/600/eea-650
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: Completion of all prior courses in the program of study.

Description:
Through the Research Capstone Project for the M.Ed degree in Leadership in Education students demonstrate the application of skills and knowledge. Students demonstrate scholarly expertise in the subject area and understanding of how scholarship is applied to and in formed by practice.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Apply leadership theory to practice.
2. Evaluate leadership integrity.
3. Develop visionary, ethical, and strategic leadership.
4. Propose applicable recommendations for leaders.
5. Design a valuable scholarly written product.

Course Code: EAD611
Title: EAD 611 Capstone: Adult Education and Instructional Design
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ead-education-adult-education/600/ead-611
Credits: 6
Prerequisites: None

Description:
The capstone in adult education and instructional design will allow students to focus on an area of interest in adult education and then create an applied project or a thesis based on research they will do on the educational modality and context.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Reflect on the process of designing an adult education program.
2. Propose a program for adult learners that addresses a real-world issue or problem applying foundational knowledge in adult education theory.
3. Integrate adult education theories, concepts, and practices to address a real-world issue or problem in adult education or service-learning.

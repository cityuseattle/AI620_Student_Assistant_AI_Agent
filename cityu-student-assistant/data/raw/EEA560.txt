Course Code: EEA560
Title: EEA 560 Leadership for Career and Technical Education
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/eea-educational-administration/500/eea-560
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course develops the candidate’s capacity for leadership in career and technical education (CTE) with the technical skills and ability to make equitable decisions related to state and federal compliance and implementation of high quality CTE programs. Candidates learn the knowledge and skills to meet part two of the Career and Technical Education (CTE) Director Competencies as published by the Washington State Professional Educator Standards Board (PESB). Candidates apply these skills through project-based assignments within a CTE program in a public school or district.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Apply best practices and state and federal compliance to develop, review, and evaluate high quality CTE programs.
2. Apply best practices and state and federal compliance to advocate for and promote high quality CTE programs.

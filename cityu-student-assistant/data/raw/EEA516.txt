Course Code: EEA516
Title: EEA 516 Professional Growth Assessment and Practicum
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/eea-educational-administration/500/eea-516
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
Candidates complete a self-assessment of their current knowledge and skills related to educational leadership. Topics covered include professional development, reflection, certification standards, professional and collegial networks. An experienced educational leader supports the candidates’ self-assessment in the knowledge, skills, and dispositions for school leadership, analysis of their instructional context, and development of a Professional Growth Plan for school and/or program leadership.

Program Information
This course will prepare students to:
1. Assess the professional environment through multiple lenses.
2. Explain and apply the essential elements of the professional standards.
3. Examine personal strengths and growth areas in each of the strands of the professional standards.

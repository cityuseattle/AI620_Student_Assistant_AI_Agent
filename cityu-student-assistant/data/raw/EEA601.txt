Course Code: EEA601
Title: EEA 601 Educational Leadership Portfolio, Presentation, and Action Research
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/eea-educational-administration/600/eea-601
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
The portfolio provides quality evidence for a specific level of competency in relationship to the program standards and is a purposeful collection of resources and documents, including action research, that records academic learning, professional development, and career accomplishments. The portfolio and its successful presentation fulfill the capstone requirement for the M.Ed. in Leadership.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Demonstrate the program outcomes through an electronic degree portfolio, presentation and action research project.

Course Code: PSY300
Title: PSY 300 Research, Reflections and Applications
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/psy-psychology/300/psy-300
Credits: 2
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course provides international students an introduction to the clinical and professional subfields that comprise the broad field of psychology in the United States. Emphasis is placed on developing an understanding of the range of psychology-related fields and services, diversity of clients and patients, and fundamental job and skill requirements for those working in these fields and services. Students will develop a more comprehensive understanding of how their educational and professional goals fit within this broad field of psychology, both in Mexico and internationally.

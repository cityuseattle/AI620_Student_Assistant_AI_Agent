Course Code: EDLD680
Title: EDLD 680 Pre-Internship Assessment and Assessment Seminar
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/edld-education-leadership/600/edld-680
Credits: 2
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This seminar begins the Superintendent Certification Program’s internship experience.  Candidates complete a developmental assessment and draft a professional growth plan in preparation for the internship.  Interns, mentors, and internship coaches engage in collaborative activities to finalize the professional growth plan for Internship I, II, and III.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Uses data and written reflections to analyze accomplishments, strengths, and needs, in order to develop goals and activities.
2. Utilize, analyze, and record data about the student’s leadership experiences.
3. Analyze personal/professional knowledge, skills, and dispositions for system leadership.

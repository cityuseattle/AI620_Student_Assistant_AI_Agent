Course Code: CPC603
Title: CPC 603 Research Project/Thesis *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/cpc-counselling-psychology-canada/600/cpc-603
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: Students in this course will have successfully completed the Comprehensive Examination (CPC 550 British Columbia, CPC 551 Alberta).

Description:
This course guides the student through the steps of producing a major scholarly document. The product of the course is a thesis, which is the culmination of a student's learning in Counselling Psychology. The structure of scientific and professional writing will be used to document original research, a clinical project, or a research proposal.

Outcomes:
None

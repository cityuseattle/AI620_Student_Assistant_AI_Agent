Course Code: IS401
Title: IS 401 eCommerce Systems
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/is-information-systems/400/is-401
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: For students to succeed in this course, IS 360 is a required pre-requisite

Description:
This course introduces the world of the web and cloud-based information technology managing cloud services, web content, and e-commerce.  It examines the opportunities, challenges, and ethical questions brought by the digital age. As an elective for any majors or a preparatory course for the bachelor's degree programs in management, it develops students' skills in commonly used applications.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Outline the fundamental concepts of Information Technologies (IT) – the Internet, web, and e-commerce.
2. Apply the fundamental concepts of IT to online store development.
3. Analyze information technologies of a Content Management System and an e-commerce package.
4. Evaluate the features of an e-commerce site for online store development.
5. Create an e-commerce site for an online store.

Course Code: ECU582
Title: ECU 582 Content & Resources in STEM and Science Education *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ecu-education-core-graduate/500/ecu-582
Credits: 6
Prerequisites: Admission to the M.Ed. program.

Description:
This course introduces candidates to content and resources in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) education. The course emphasizes building science content knowledge and applying that knowledge to instruction in the K-12 classroom. Candidates learn about Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS), and the four areas of NGSS: Life Science, Physical Science, Earth & Space Science, and Engineering & Technology.  Candidates learn about scientific investigations, inquiry-based learning, and teaching science based on anchoring phenomena.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Synthesize knowledge of STEM content by planning multi-dimensional instruction.
2. Anticipate and plan for students’ alternative conceptions/misconceptions related to STEM content.
3. Demonstrate leadership in STEM and science education.
4. Evaluate and apply research and theory related to best practices in STEM education.
5. Demonstrate a commitment to equity and culturally responsive teaching in STEM education.

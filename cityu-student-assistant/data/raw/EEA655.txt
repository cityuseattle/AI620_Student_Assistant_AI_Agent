Course Code: EEA655
Title: EEA 655 Thesis and Portfolio Presentation *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/eea-educational-administration/600/eea-655
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: Completion of all prior courses in the program of study.

Description:
The thesis for the M.Ed. degree in Educational Leadership demonstrates the application of skills and knowledge designed to address a "Problem Statement" in an educational setting and fulfills the capstone requirement for the M.Ed. in Educational Leadership. Candidates research a problem related to school leadership in research involving human subjects. The course outcome is a thesis of 50+ pages that includes all aspects of the design, execution and analysis of a research project using accepted methodology.

Outcomes:
None

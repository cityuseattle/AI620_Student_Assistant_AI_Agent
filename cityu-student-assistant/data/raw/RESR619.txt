Course Code: RESR619
Title: RESR 619 Quantitative Research Methods
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/resr-research/600/resr-619
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course covers quantitative methodology and design. Equal emphasis will be placed on the fundamental elements of: (1) quantitative design including variable identification, research question and hypothesis creation; and (2) quantitative data collection and analysis including sampling methods, data collection instruments, and an introduction to statistical tests.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Explain the fundamental elements of quantitative methodology and design.
2. Critically evaluate the purpose, design, data analysis methods, and findings of quantitative studies in published articles.
3. Develop a quantitative research proposal.

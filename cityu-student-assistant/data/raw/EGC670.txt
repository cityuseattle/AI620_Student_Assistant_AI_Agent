Course Code: EGC670
Title: EGC 670 Applied Group Counselling in Schools *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/egc-professional-school-counseling/600/egc-670
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: EGC 536

Description:
This course applies theoretical constructs from EGC 536 to master group counselling facilitation in an experiential group counselling laboratory. Candidates apply group process theory and skills by participating as members, facilitators and observers in counselling groups. Practice and application of theory and skills establishes the candidate as an effective group leader and facilitator.

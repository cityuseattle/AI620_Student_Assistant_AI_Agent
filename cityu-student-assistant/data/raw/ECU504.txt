Course Code: ECU504
Title: ECU 504 Graduate Research and Methodology *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ecu-education-core-graduate/500/ecu-504
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: Admission to the M.Ed. program.

Description:
The Research and Methodology block contains outcomes relating to the understanding, critical application, and value of current research processes and how these increase student learning. Candidates gain an understanding of the various types of research and purpose for them in improving educational practices. Candidates submit various artifacts which demonstrate their understanding of and ability to implement best research practices that enhance student learning, meet individual needs and improve educational practices.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Identify characteristics of scholarly research (e.g., methodology, methods, etc.).
2. Develop an appropriate research question.
3. Differentiate between high-quality and low-quality research.
4. Evaluate if and how scholarly sources are relevant to a particular research question.
5. Synthesize multiple scholarly sources into a literature review.

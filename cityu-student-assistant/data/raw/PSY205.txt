Course Code: PSY205
Title: PSY 205 Lifespan Developmental Psychology *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/psy-psychology/200/psy-205
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: Only available in Canada.

Description:
This course provides an introduction to developmental psychology, exploring the psychological growth and changes that occur throughout the human lifespan. Key theories and research on cognitive, emotional, social, and physical development will be examined, with an emphasis on understanding how these developmental processes interact and influence each other.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Synthesize key developmental theories (Cognitive Development in Childhood Paper).
2. Analyze psychosocial factors in identity formation (Identity Formation in Adolescence Paper).
3. Assess the role of meaning-making and emotions (Meaning, Relationships, and Emotions Presentation/Project).
4. Evaluate the impact of relationships on development (Meaning, Relationships, and Emotions Presentation/Project).

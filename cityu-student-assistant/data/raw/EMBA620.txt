Course Code: EMBA620
Title: EMBA 620 Residency III
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/emba-executive-mba/600/emba-620
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
The capstone residency class is the final course in the EMBA program and is designed to help students integrate the knowledge and skills they have gained throughout the program into a real-world business project. Through the use of workshops, case studies, simulations and guest speakers, this class provides an opportunity for students to demonstrate their mastery of key concepts and to apply their knowledge to solve complex business challenges.  In addition to project work, the capstone residency often includes opportunities for networking and professional development. Students have the opportunity to meet with alumni and industry leaders, participate in career development workshops, and attend panel discussions on topics relevant to their career goals.

Course Code: COM397
Title: COM 397 Practicum Preparation and Presentation Skills (HU)
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/com-communications-revised/300/com-397
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: None

Description:
With the Communications-Social Media degree program culminating in a practicum project designed to use media technologies, theories, and communication strategies, tools, and practices, this course provides an orientation for students to design and plan their unique project. Portfolio development and presentation techniques integral to the practicum project, including the requirement to present the practicum experience and results through social technology, will be addressed. Students will build skills to form and deliver presentations and content-specific messages through current technological applications.

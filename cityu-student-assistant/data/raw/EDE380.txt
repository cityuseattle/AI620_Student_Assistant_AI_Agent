Course Code: EDE380
Title: EDE 380 Social Studies
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ede-education-elementary/300/ede-380
Credits: 4
Prerequisites: None

Description:
In this course, candidates investigate various teaching strategies to motivate children, explore what it means to critically evaluate sources, interpret events, and promote civic behavior and discourse. This course introduces candidates to methods of social studies instruction with an emphasis on understanding diverse perspectives and interpretations of events in history and helps candidates further develop the skills to integrate multiple content areas.

Program Information
This course will prepare students to:
1. Identify and develop instructional best practices in Social Studies.
2. Identify the four major areas of Social Studies.
3. Implement state standards and Since Time Immemorial curriculum into a Social Studies learning segment.

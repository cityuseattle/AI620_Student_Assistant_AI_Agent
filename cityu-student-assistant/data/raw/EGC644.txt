Course Code: EGC644
Title: EGC 644 Pro Cert Core Seminar II *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/egc-professional-school-counseling/600/egc-644
Credits: 1
Prerequisites: Successful completion of EGC 642 Pro Cert Entry Seminar and EGC 643 Core Seminar I.

Description:
Pro Cert Seminar II is based on the requirements of the Professional Educator Standards Board (PESB). Candidate completes a self-assessment (professional audit) and identifies evidence(s) which demonstrate competency at the professional level for ESA Standards 5B Student Learning and Assessment and 5C Counseling Theories & Techniques. Candidate submits the first sections of the action research narrative: introduction and proposed intervention in SMART goal format with data collection strategies. Candidate initiates data collection for the action research project.

None

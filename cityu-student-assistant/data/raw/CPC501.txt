Course Code: CPC501
Title: CPC 501 Introduction to Counselling Psychology Practice *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/cpc-counselling-psychology-canada/500/cpc-501
Credits: 5
Prerequisites: Students are not permitted to challenge this course or undertake it as an independent learning contract (ILC).

Description:
This introductory course explores ways that counsellors can assist others in developing preferred lives and identities. Students will develop their own unique ways of engaging with others toward therapeutic ends. This course will focus on the safe exploration of therapeutic conversations. Guidelines on appropriate personal disclosure will be provided by the instructor.

Course Code: LDRD662
Title: LDRD 662 Student Populations and Experiences
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/ldrd-leadership/600/ldrd-662
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course explores student populations and how students experience the higher education institution. Emphasis is placed on applying student development theory across the diversity of student populations to gain a deep understanding of the student experience. Students will evaluate how higher education changes students.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Analyze emerging trends in enrollment demographics in higher education.
Evaluate how higher education changes students and the role that the institution plays in shaping that change.
Examine theories and models that describe the development of college students and the conditions that impact development.

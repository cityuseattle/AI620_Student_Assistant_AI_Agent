Course Code: MAL568
Title: MAL 568 Financial Management of Nonprofit Organizations
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/mal-management-leadership/500/mal-568
Credits: 3
Prerequisites: None

Description:
This course provides a foundation for effective financial management to promote the financial sustainability of the nonprofit organization. Emphasis will be placed on understanding financial systems as they apply to the nonprofit organization; evaluation of the financial health of the nonprofit; and strategies to improve the financial condition of the nonprofit organization including budgeting, cash management and banking, financial reporting, purchasing and contracting, borrowing and risk management.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
Analyze the financial health of a nonprofit organization.
Examine the issues affecting the fiscal management of a nonprofit organization.
Develop procedures to ensure effective internal controls in a nonprofit organization.

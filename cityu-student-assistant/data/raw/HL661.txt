Course Code: HL661
Title: HL 661 Capstone - Practicum *
URL: https://cityu.smartcatalogiq.com/en/2025-2026/2025-2026-catalog/course-descriptions/hl-healthcare/600/hl-661
Credits: 6
Prerequisites: This course is the practicum capstone for students in the Master of Science in Healthcare Administration. This course is the final course in the program and should not be taken until the student has completed all other coursework.

Description:
This healthcare administration practicum capstone course provides students with healthcare-related learning, either through an experiential project or a written research article. Whether through a project-based experience within a healthcare environment or a written research paper that focuses on a healthcare-related problem, students have the opportunity gain specific healthcare related skills. Deliverables for an experiential project are contingent upon the site but may include project reports, reflection papers, and evaluations by their site supervisor. Deliverables for the research paper may include an identification of a root problem and potential solutions with recommendations. By the end of the course students have developed a deeper understanding of the field of healthcare administration.

Outcomes:
This course will prepare students to:
1. Examine unique projects that contribute to the knowledge of the healthcare administration field.
2. Apply business theories, models, frameworks, and/or strategies to identified healthcare administration organizational problems.
3. Analyze healthcare administration problems or challenges and recommend solutions.
